How The Blood Request Process Works

Our blood request process is designed to be simple and efficient:

  1. Fill out the request form with your personal information and blood needs
  2. Select your preferred location for pickup
  3. Verify your eligibility through our verification process
  4. Receive confirmation of your request
  5. Visit the designated location with proper identification

Most requests are processed within 24-48 hours depending on availability.

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible to request blood products, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Provide a valid government-issued photo ID
  • Present a doctor's prescription or medical documentation
  • Complete all required forms and agreements

Additional documentation may be required depending on your specific situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does requesting blood cost?

While blood itself is often provided free of charge, there may be processing fees associated with testing, storage, and distribution. These fees vary by location and circumstances.

How long does the process take?

The initial request process takes 15-20 minutes to complete. Processing time varies depending on availability and urgency, but typically takes 24-48 hours.

Can I request blood for someone else?

Yes, you can request blood for a family member or patient under your care, but you will need to provide their medical documentation and proof of your relationship or authority.
