How Blood Donation Works

Step 1: Registration

Sign up online or at a donation center. You'll need to provide identification and complete a brief health questionnaire.

Step 2: Health Screening

Undergo a mini health check including temperature, pulse, blood pressure, and hemoglobin test to ensure you're eligible to donate.

Step 3: Donation

The actual donation takes only about 8-10 minutes. You'll relax in a comfortable chair while approximately one pint of blood is collected.

Step 4: Refreshments

After donating, you'll rest and enjoy refreshments for 15 minutes to help your body recover before resuming your day.

Time Commitment

The entire process takes about 1 hour from start to finish, with the actual blood drawing only taking 8-10 minutes.
